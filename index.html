If no one stops to appreciate and remember each star, they fade into the darkness - worthless, like the grains of sand that we walk on.

"Home is not a place, it is people."
- Lois McMaster

... As I journey through life, join me in seeing grains of sand that make the journey bearable and the stars that make my nights colorful.
